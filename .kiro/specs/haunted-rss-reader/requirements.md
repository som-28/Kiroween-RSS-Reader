# Requirements Document

## Introduction

The Haunted RSS Reader is a modern resurrection of RSS feed technology, enhanced with AI-powered features and wrapped in a spooky Halloween-themed interface. The system brings dead RSS technology back to life by combining classic feed reading capabilities with cutting-edge AI summarization, intelligent curation, and an unforgettable haunted user experience. This application demonstrates how obsolete technology can be reimagined to solve modern information overload problems while delivering a visually striking, themed interface.

## Glossary

- **Feed Reader**: The core application system that fetches, parses, and displays RSS/Atom feeds
- **AI Curator**: The machine learning component that analyzes, summarizes, and categorizes feed content
- **Feed Source**: An external RSS/Atom feed URL that provides syndicated content
- **Article**: An individual content item from a Feed Source
- **Digest**: A curated collection of summarized Articles generated by the AI Curator
- **Haunted UI**: The Halloween-themed user interface with spooky visual elements
- **Smart Filter**: An AI-powered content filtering mechanism based on user preferences
- **Audio Summary**: Text-to-speech generated audio version of article summaries

## Requirements

### Requirement 1

**User Story:** As a user, I want to add and manage RSS feed sources, so that I can aggregate content from multiple sources in one place

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a user provides a valid RSS/Atom feed URL, THE Feed Reader SHALL fetch and parse the feed content
2. WHEN a user adds a Feed Source, THE Feed Reader SHALL validate the URL format and feed structure before saving
3. THE Feed Reader SHALL display all subscribed Feed Sources in a manageable list with options to edit or remove
4. WHEN a Feed Source fails to load, THE Feed Reader SHALL display an error message with the specific failure reason
5. THE Feed Reader SHALL support both RSS 2.0 and Atom 1.0 feed formats

### Requirement 2

**User Story:** As a user, I want AI-generated summaries of articles, so that I can quickly understand content without reading full articles

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN an Article is fetched from a Feed Source, THE AI Curator SHALL generate a concise summary within 10 seconds
2. THE AI Curator SHALL limit summaries to a maximum of 150 words while preserving key information
3. WHEN a user views an Article, THE Feed Reader SHALL display both the AI-generated summary and original content
4. THE AI Curator SHALL extract and display key topics and entities from each Article
5. IF the AI Curator fails to generate a summary, THEN THE Feed Reader SHALL display the original article excerpt

### Requirement 3

**User Story:** As a user, I want personalized content filtering, so that I only see articles relevant to my interests

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Feed Reader SHALL allow users to specify interest topics and keywords for content filtering
2. WHEN new Articles are fetched, THE Smart Filter SHALL score each Article based on user preferences
3. THE Feed Reader SHALL display Articles sorted by relevance score in descending order
4. WHEN a user marks an Article as interesting or not interesting, THE Smart Filter SHALL update preference weights
5. THE Smart Filter SHALL learn from user interactions and improve filtering accuracy over time

### Requirement 4

**User Story:** As a user, I want audio summaries of articles, so that I can consume content while multitasking

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a user requests an audio version, THE Feed Reader SHALL generate an Audio Summary from the AI-generated text summary
2. THE Feed Reader SHALL provide playback controls including play, pause, and speed adjustment
3. THE Audio Summary SHALL complete generation within 5 seconds of user request
4. THE Feed Reader SHALL allow users to queue multiple Audio Summaries for continuous playback
5. WHEN Audio Summary generation fails, THE Feed Reader SHALL display an error notification and fallback to text display

### Requirement 5

**User Story:** As a user, I want a spooky Halloween-themed interface, so that the app is visually memorable and engaging

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Haunted UI SHALL use a dark color palette with Halloween-themed colors including deep purples, oranges, and blacks
2. THE Haunted UI SHALL include animated spooky elements such as floating ghosts, flickering candles, or cobweb effects
3. WHEN a user interacts with UI elements, THE Haunted UI SHALL provide themed hover effects and transitions
4. THE Haunted UI SHALL use Halloween-appropriate fonts and iconography throughout the application
5. THE Haunted UI SHALL maintain readability and usability despite decorative themed elements

### Requirement 6

**User Story:** As a user, I want daily or weekly digests of curated content, so that I can catch up on important articles efficiently

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE AI Curator SHALL generate a Digest containing the most relevant Articles based on user preferences
2. WHEN a scheduled digest time occurs, THE Feed Reader SHALL compile and display the Digest automatically
3. THE Feed Reader SHALL allow users to configure digest frequency as daily, weekly, or on-demand
4. THE Digest SHALL include a maximum of 10 Articles with summaries and relevance explanations
5. THE Feed Reader SHALL send a notification when a new Digest is available

### Requirement 7

**User Story:** As a user, I want to discover connections between articles from different feeds, so that I can understand broader topics and trends

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN Articles share common topics or entities, THE AI Curator SHALL identify and display these connections
2. THE Feed Reader SHALL visualize related Articles in a grouped or linked format
3. THE AI Curator SHALL detect trending topics across multiple Feed Sources
4. WHEN a user views an Article, THE Feed Reader SHALL display related Articles from other Feed Sources
5. THE Feed Reader SHALL update topic connections in real-time as new Articles are fetched

### Requirement 8

**User Story:** As a user, I want the app to work offline with cached content, so that I can read articles without an internet connection

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Feed Reader SHALL cache fetched Articles and their summaries in local storage
2. WHEN the user is offline, THE Feed Reader SHALL display all cached content with an offline indicator
3. THE Feed Reader SHALL synchronize new content automatically when internet connectivity is restored
4. THE Feed Reader SHALL allow users to manually mark Articles for offline availability
5. WHEN storage limits are reached, THE Feed Reader SHALL remove oldest cached Articles based on a least-recently-used policy

### Requirement 9

**User Story:** As a user, I want smart notifications for high-priority content, so that I don't miss important articles

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN an Article matches high-priority user preferences, THE Feed Reader SHALL send a notification within 1 minute of fetching
2. THE Feed Reader SHALL allow users to configure notification preferences including frequency and priority thresholds
3. THE Feed Reader SHALL include the Article title and AI-generated summary in the notification
4. WHEN a user clicks a notification, THE Feed Reader SHALL navigate directly to the relevant Article
5. THE Feed Reader SHALL respect system-level notification settings and do-not-disturb modes

### Requirement 10

**User Story:** As a user, I want to search through my feed history, so that I can find previously read articles

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Feed Reader SHALL provide a search interface that queries Article titles, content, and summaries
2. WHEN a user enters a search query, THE Feed Reader SHALL return results within 2 seconds
3. THE Feed Reader SHALL support filtering search results by date range, Feed Source, and topics
4. THE Feed Reader SHALL highlight matching keywords in search results
5. THE Feed Reader SHALL display search results sorted by relevance with most recent Articles prioritized
